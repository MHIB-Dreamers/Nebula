Conquering the Planetary Galaxy is an engaging educational game that helps players learn different languages through the exciting journey of a cosmic cat. Players will explore various planets, each representing a unique language and culture.

Language Story

English

Azure Planet - where everyone communicates in English and experiences Anglo-American culture. Rich vocabulary and diverse grammar help learners access the global language.

Chinese

Amber Planet - where ancient and modern Chinese characters blend. Learn about Chinese writing, pronunciation, and popular cultural expressions.

Korean

Magenta Planet - land of Hangul. Discover Korean language with its unique writing system, pronunciation, and popular cultural elements.

Vietnamese

Jade Planet - preserving the sound of Vietnamese. Learn about tone marks, intonation, and unique Vietnamese idioms and proverbs.
